Fried Chicken Recipe

This recipe is finger-licking good and will have your family dying for more.

    The Ingredients you will need are:

  • One pack pf chicken (Breasts, Wings or Thighs)
  • Old Bay
  • Seasoning Salt
  • Sazon
  • Onion powder
  • Garlic Powder,
  • Pepper
  • Frank's Hot Sauce
  • Mustard
  • Lemon Juice
  • Salt
    Step by Step Instruction:
  1. Clean your Chicken with 2 teaspoons of lemon juice, cold water and 2 teaspoons of salt.
  2. Starting with Old Bay, season your chicken (dry seasoning) until it is entirely covered.
  3. You will then add 5 drops of hot sauce and 2squirts of mustard to the chicken
  4. Put your enough oil in your pot for the chicken to be covered and turn it on med heat
  5. Massage your chicken to get all the seasoning marinated into the chicken.
  6. Once your oil is hot, place chicken inside until golden brown. (approx. 10-15 minutes)
